IdeaThon 2021

  1. You have to submit a video of 1 or 2 minutes explaining your idea/project.

  2. In this ATL IDEATHON you don't required to create a prototype but if you can you may.

  3. You may choose any topic but that must revolve around SMART INDIA.

  4. Some Suggested topics are- Waste management, Renewable energy, Heathcare and Biomedical, Clean water, Security and surveillance,Smart Vehicles,etc.

  5. Students are free to identify and solve ANY OTHER relevant problem aligning with SMART INDIA.

  6. The video should be UNIQUE and not copied from internet.

  7. In video formal English language should be used.

  8. Editing of video is allowed. ( You may add illustrations, research work, news articles to support your Unique idea(s)).
